Bringing More People to Fighting Games, One Video at a Time!

Hello, my name is ShaolinPretzels! I am a competitive fighting game player, content creator, and streamer, currently residing in Central Pennyslvania.

Content Creator

I am an active content creator on Twitch and YouTube. My Twitch live streams feature my tournament runs, live arcade stick builds, as well as other events and collaborations with my fellow fighting game content creators. On YouTube, I produce commentary videos discussing the topics of competitive fighting games, arcade stick modification, and greater fighting game community.

Tournament Player

Since I started competing back in 2018, I have amassed numerous top 8 and top 4 placements, with my best placement being 1st at Collegiate Star League’s first Street Fighter V tournament in 2019. I am also known for competing in titles such as Samurai Shodown, Granblue Fantasy Versus, and other major fighting game releases.

Community Figure

I am a regional authority on arcade stick modification, a former stream producer for the Ohio Fighting Game Community’s tournament series, Dragon Punch, and the co-founder of Penn FGC, the official website of the Pennsylvania Fighting Game Community.
