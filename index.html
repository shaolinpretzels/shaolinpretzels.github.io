Who Am I?

My name is Bryce Allen, though most people in my community know me as "ShaolinPretzels." I've been a tournament fighting game player since July 2018. I have numerous Top 8 and Top 16 placements across multiple games, in tournaments held in the US East Coast and Midwest, as well as in online tournaments. I'm also a video content creator and an authority in my region on the subject of arcade stick and controller modding.
