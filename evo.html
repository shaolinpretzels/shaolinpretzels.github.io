Help Me Compete At Evolution 2020!

With the help of my community, I hope to attend Evolution 2020, the world's most prestigious fighting game tournament, in Las Vegas, Nevada on July 31st to August 2nd. My aims are to compete in my chosen games and produce video content for my audience.

At present, I'm an unsponsored player and also study as a full-time student for roughly 7-8 months of the year. As such I have to be selective about which large-scale events that I can attend, because I can only attend so few per year. I want to continue to grow as a competitive player and wish to continue to put myself in environments where I can learn, grow, and connect with others. In the fighting game community, there is no better time of the year to do this than during Evolution.

I wish to test the abilities that I've acquired during my years as a competitive player, while, hopefully, providing some entertaining content for you guys as well, via my Twitter, Twitch, and YouTube profiles.

What Do You Get Out Of It?

Over the course of the event, I'd like to produce the following content for my platforms:

  • Daily vlogs
  • Reviews at the Indie Game Showcase
  • Live reactions to Sunday Finals announcements
  • More content TBD
